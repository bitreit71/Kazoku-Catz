    Faq

    What is Kazoku Catz?

    Kazoku Catz is a collection of Web 3.0 gangsters operating on the Cardano Network.

    What is the mint price?

    The mint price will be 37 ADA for each NFT

    When is the mint date?

    The mint date will be the 20th June 2022. Members with a Whitelist spot will be able to mint from 14:30 UTC to 17:00 UTC. At 17:15 UTC the public sale will commence.

    How many can be minted?

    Members with a whitelist spot will be able to mint a maximum of 3 NFTs. Members looking to mint in the public sale will be able to mint 3 per transaction.
